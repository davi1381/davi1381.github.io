About Me

I’m Aaron C. Davis, currently wrapping up my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University, where I’ve been deep into research on microrobotics for targeted drug delivery and micro-manipulation. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from BYU in 2019.

My work focuses on microfabrication, micro 3D printing, micro-assembly, and magnetic manipulation. I’ve designed and tested several magnetic microrobots, leading to multiple publications and conference presentations. It’s exciting to see how these tiny devices can make a big impact, especially in healthcare.
